Simple billing for quick turnaround payments

With Atlas patients are alerted they have a bill via text message and email, and can pay directly from their phone in two taps.

Current Problem

Patients don't want paper bills in the mail. They want to pay the same way they do everything else: online or on their phone. Even if a patient opts for paper billing, they often look like junk mail and get tossed aside.

Incentivize payment with timed discounts

Atlas enables providers to offer prompt-pay discounts to encourage and incentivize patients to "pay now.”  Studies have shown this tactic dramatically increases revenue and cash flow.

Current Problem

There's no direct penalty for not paying a medical bill – only a potential ding against one's credit score six months down the road. Without penalties or incentives, patients' don't feel any urgency to pay their bills on time.

More comfort with payment installment options

Atlas allows providers to offer patient payment plans that automatically deduct cash from the patient's bank account on a monthly basis until the bill is paid in full. For certain bills and patients this is the only way to recover payment.

Current Problem

Sometimes patients simply don't have the money to pay the bill in full. As a result, they don't pay anything.

Peace of mind with bill verification

Atlas’ error scanning technology is applied to every bill before it reaches the patient's phone and inbox, giving them peace of mind that the bill is verified and ready to be paid.

Current Problem

Medical bills often contain mistakes due to incorrect patient information, insurance coverage issues, and coding complexity. As a result, patients are afraid to pay.

All day support for any outstanding questions

Atlas’ chatbot AI technology offers 24/7 in-app chat support to resolve questions in real time so patients can pay and go about their day.

Current Problem

Many times Patients often have questions about their bill but don't have the time or desire to call for help.
